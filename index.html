Mission

Essential medicines require essential infrastructure.

Genhtis exists to secure the supply of critical pharmaceuticals for Western healthcare systems. We are building infrastructure-grade manufacturing capacity for medicines that hospitals cannot operate without.

Supply security is not a logistics problem. It is a strategic imperative.

The Challenge

Western healthcare depends on fragile supply chains.

01

Concentration Risk

Critical pharmaceutical ingredients are sourced from a small number of regions. A single disruption can cascade across entire healthcare systems.

02

No Strategic Reserve

Most essential medicines have no domestic or allied-nation manufacturing base. Stockpiles are measured in weeks, not months.

03

Systemic Vulnerability

Geopolitical tension, trade restrictions, and quality incidents have already caused shortages. The risk profile is increasing.

Our Approach

Infrastructure-grade manufacturing. Built for resilience.

Vertically Integrated

Genhtis controls the full manufacturing chain. From raw input to finished pharmaceutical product, every step is secured and auditable.

Western-Based Production

Manufacturing facilities located in allied nations. No dependency on adversarial or unstable supply corridors.

Scalable by Design

Modular manufacturing architecture enables rapid capacity expansion. Built to meet surge demand when continuity matters most.

First Focus

Heparin.

Heparin is one of the most widely used anticoagulants in modern medicine. It is essential for cardiac surgery, dialysis, trauma care, and critical interventions across every major hospital system. Today, the global supply of heparin raw material is concentrated in a single region, creating a strategic vulnerability that affects millions of patients annually.

Genhtis has selected heparin as its first critical medicine. A proven need. A clear vulnerability. A solvable problem.
